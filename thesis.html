2005 GRADUATION THESIS SHOWCASE

Design with Animals
Dynamic Structure Design

A contemporary architectural translation and blueprint reconstruction of the biological motion trajectories, structural mechanics, and kinetic envelopes of Gibbons, Kangaroos, and Giraffes. 以長臂猿、袋鼠及長頸鹿的運動軌跡與生物力學骨骼系統為靈感，現代化重塑的動態構造設計與仿生建築學術實踐。

MASTER'S DEGREE THESIS / 碩士學位論文

Tamkang University, Taiwan (2004) / 淡江大學建築學系研究所

Abstract: Animal profile and movement have a long term impact on human history and inspire numerous designs from product to architecture. This is a study focus on using computer animation technology in the design process. Movement of animals is used as a simulation target to create a kinematic design from small to large scale such as architecture. Giraffe, Gibbon, and Kangaroo are chosen as the study resource for developing the final design. The final design intends to connect the environment with kinematic devices to create a sustainable solution. Because of the continuous change environment, the direction of creating kinematic architecture skin should properly respond to Mother Nature.

論文摘要： 動物的輪廓與運動方式對人類歷史有著深遠的影響，並啟發了從產品到建築的無數設計。本研究重點在於將電腦動畫技術應用於設計流程中，以動物運動軌跡作為模擬目標，創造出從微觀到宏觀（如建築尺度）的動態可動構造設計。本案選取長頸鹿、長臂猿與袋鼠作為開發終期動態方案的研究對象，旨在透過可動構造機制連結建築皮層與周遭環境，創造永續的空間解決方案。面對不斷變遷的自然環境，動態建築皮層的設計方向應能妥善地對大自然做出回應。

01

The Dynamic Canopy (M)
捷運入口連續擺動頂棚

Origin: Giraffe Collapsible Neck Motion

Design Concept: Simulates the collapsible drinking motion of a giraffe's neck. Translating a single pivoting structural element into a sequence of 20 adjacent beams. By programming a sequential phase delay in the rotation axes, the beams generate a fluid, wave-like spatial canopy surface over the metro entrance stairs.

設計概念： 模擬長頸鹿喝水時頸部向下俯衝的開合姿態。將單根懸臂桿件複製為 20 組等距排列的排桿，並藉由參數化時間差控制，使相鄰桿件產生連續的相位偏轉，進而在捷運階梯入口上方形塑出如水面波浪般流暢的動態遮陰曲面。

02

The Dynamic Bus Stop (M)
公車站候車亭主動遮陽頂棚

Origin: Gibbon Pendulum Swing Motion

Design Concept: Built as an active solar-shading envelope. Incorporating a sun position sensor to pivot the shading louvers synchronously with the solar path. This system continuously optimizes the shading angle, guaranteeing that the shadow cast remains centered over the passenger seating area throughout the day.

設計概念： 結合主動追日遮陽概念，模擬長臂猿擺盪擺軸活動機制。透過感應裝置追蹤太陽高度角與方位角，動態調節候車亭頂棚的格柵傾斜角度，使投影遮陰面積始終覆蓋於等車旅客的座位區，達成全天候最佳隔熱遮陰功效。

03

The Kangaroo Bookstore (L)
袋鼠跳躍折疊遮陽立面

Origin: Kangaroo Spring-Loaded Joints

Design Concept: Explores the spring-loaded energy conservation in kangaroo jumping. Translating mechanical scissor-linkages and spring dampers into foldable facade panels. The storefront awnings expand and compress in a wave-like sequence to adapt to sun angles, ventilation requirements, or storefront open hours.

設計概念： 探討袋鼠跳躍時雙腿關節的彈簧儲能特性。將連桿活塞與伸縮阻尼轉化為立面的剪刀腳機械關節。書店外的立面遮陽板可如波浪般規律壓縮與折疊，靈活適應四季的進光量、自然通風需求與書店營業狀態。
