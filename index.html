PHILOSOPHY

Our Design
Philosophy

We believe that exceptional architecture is not defined by expensive construction costs, but by meaningful space arrangements, precise proportions, contrast of materials and colors, and elegant detailing. 我們深信，好的建築不取決於昂貴的營建成本，而在於空間配置的意義、精準的比例、材料與色彩的對比，以及細部的巧思。

Through our "ModuleCell" pre-fab system, we standardize these design values to make premium modern spaces accessible, comfortable, and sustainable. 透過「ModuleCell」預製單元系統，我們將這樣的設計精神商品化與預製化，讓高品質的現代居住空間觸手可及。

WORKFLOW

The Lab & Design Workflow / 實作工坊與設計流程

Revit Parametric Cricut Cutting 3D Printing Twinmotion & Animation

Bridging Imagination & Reality through Craft and Tech
用雙手與科技拉近夢想的距離

At ModuleCell, we design beyond the screen. We have established a robust "digital-to-physical" prototyping workflow to ensure high-fidelity design execution: 在 ModuleCell，我們不只在螢幕前做設計。我們開發了一套「從數位到物理原型」的完整工作流，確保設計的完美實踐：

  • 01
    Revit Parametric Library / 參數化 Revit 族群開發： Developing standardized wall modules and hardware libraries to guarantee drawing precision and constructability. 建立標準化牆體模組與五金節點參數庫，確保圖紙的高精準度與現場組裝的可行性。
  • 02
    Cricut Cardboard Cutting / Cricut 精密紙模型切割： Exporting 2D Revit layouts to automated cutters to assemble fine cardboard models for checking spatial proportions under real light. 將 Revit 平立面導出至自動切割機，組裝精製的卡紙比例模型，在實際日光下驗證空間比例與遮陽夾角。
  • 03
    3D Printed Prototypes / 3D 列印快速原型： Printing complex joints and custom structural pieces to provide tactile demonstrations of physical assembly interfaces. 將複雜的幾何單元或節點配件進行 3D 列印，提供給業主與工廠可觸摸、可拆裝的物理卡榫原型。
  • 04
    Twinmotion & Stop-motion / 渲染與定格動畫： Generating photo-real visuals and assembly animations to align spatial execution with contractors and clients. 產出高擬真渲染效果圖與裝配縮時影片，讓施工團隊與業主完全同步未來的空間實景與組裝步驟。
